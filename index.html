I am a graduate student at Nanjing University since 2021. And my advisor is Prof. Gang Tian.

My research interest lies in complex algebraic geometry and complex differential geometry. Currently, I am applying algebraic K-stability theory to questions about Kähler-Ricci Flow and Kähler-Ricci soliton on Fano variety. Here is my cv.
