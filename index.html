Kewei Zhang 张科伟

kwzhang@bnu.edu.cn, kwzhang@pku.edu.cn

I am an associate professor at Beijing Normal University, since 2024.

I was a postdoc at the Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research during 2019-2021.

I completed my PhD in 2019 at Peking University, China.

My interest lies in complex geometry, algebriac geometry and geometric PDE.
