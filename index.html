I am a fourth year PhD student at Nanjing University since 2021. And my advisor is Prof. Gang Tian.

My research interest lies in complex algebraic geometry and complex differential geometry.

Currently, I am applying algebraic K-stability theory to questions about Kähler-Ricci Flow and Kähler-Ricci soliton on Fano varieties. Here is my CV.
